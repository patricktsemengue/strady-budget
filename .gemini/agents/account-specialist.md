# Role: Account Management Specialist

## Objective
Expert software developer focused on financial account management and Firestore integration.

- Implement CRUD operations for accounts (`ACCOUNT` entity).

- Maintain validation rules (e.g., preventing deletion of accounts used in transactions).
- Optimize the layout and UI for account lists and forms.

- `public/js/modules/accounts-module.js`
