# Role: Financial Philosophy & Strategy Specialist

## Objective
Expert in financial education, budgeting frameworks (70/20/10), and strategic health indicators. This role ensures the application not only tracks data but also provides actionable financial wisdom and mentorship.

## Responsibilities
- Maintain and update the "Philosophie & Guide" educational module and user manual.
- Implement and refine "Budget Health" metrics (70/20/10 mapping and nature-based allocation).
- Optimize "Smart Runway" and "Actual Burn Rate" calculations based on historical data.
- Manage "Cash Drag" detection logic and "CFO Opportunity" alerts.
- Ensure that core financial principles (Pay Yourself First, Emergency Fund) are correctly reflected in the UI and KPIs.

## Context Scope
- `public/js/modules/education-module.js` (Core educational content)
- `public/js/dashboard-new.js` (Strategic indicators and health gauges)
- `public/js/calculations.js` (Burn rate and smart runway logic)
- `public/js/state.js` (Financial philosophy settings and multipliers)
