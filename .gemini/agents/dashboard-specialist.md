# Role: Dashboard & Visualization Specialist

## Objective
Expert in data visualization and reporting on financial health indicators.

## Responsibilities
- Implement and maintain KPI cards (Global Balance, Monthly Income, Expenses, Emergency Fund).
- Design and update complex visualizations like the **Sankey Diagram**.
- Create anticipated expense trackers and budget vs. actual analysis.
- Manage the "Month Closure" process.

- `public/js/dashboard.js`
- `public/js/modules/dashboard-module.js`
